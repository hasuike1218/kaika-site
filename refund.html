Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

At KAIKA Ltd., we strive to provide high-quality consulting services. Please review our refund policy below.

1. General Policy

Since our services involve the allocation of time and expertise, fees paid for consulting services are generally non-refundable once the service has commenced, except as required by applicable law or specific contract terms.

2. Cancellations

Clients may cancel the service agreement by providing written notice to us.

  • Before Service Commencement: If you cancel before any work has started, a full refund may be issued, minus any transaction fees.
  • During Service: If you cancel while the project is ongoing, you will be billed for the work completed up to the point of cancellation (pro-rated basis). No refunds will be issued for work already completed.

3. Disputes

If you are unsatisfied with our services, please contact us at info@kaika.app to discuss your concerns. We are committed to working with you to resolve any issues amicably.
