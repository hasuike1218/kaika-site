KAIKA Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website and services.

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name and Contact information (Email address, Phone number)

We also automatically collect technical information through cookies and tracking technologies (see Section 3).

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Tag Manager (GTM), services provided by Google LLC, to analyze how visitors use our website. These tools automatically collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring websites and traffic sources
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level)

This data is collected anonymously and processed by Google LLC in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

You may opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it only with trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors, hosting, analytics providers such as Google LLC) or when required by law.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
